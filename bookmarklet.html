About This Tutorial

For testing purposes, the next version of MBooks can be accessed via the development server. Because this isn't a "live" site, the procedure for using it is a little more complicated than it would normally be. These instructions are only intended to get you started, not to describe how to do things - you're on your own for that.

 

Building Collections in MBooks Dev

Method 1: Copy items from existing MBooks collections

  1. Open MBooks Collection interface: http://dev-linux.umdl.umich.edu/cgi/mb
  2. You will be prompted to login
  3. You'll see a list of "Public Collections," choose one by clicking the collection's title.
  4. You'll see a list of items within your chosen collection...

Method 2: Find items in Mirlyn to add to MBooks

(this is where it gets a tad complicated)
  1. Save the bookmarklet link to your toolbar/links list (see instructions below)
  2. When viewing a record in Mirlyn where the MBooks icon is displayed, click on the bookmarklet
  3. This will take you to the item in MBooks where you can click on "Add to your collection"

Alternatively, you can paste the barcode number of any mdp item into this url (paste over the last part of the url - starting with 39015). A book's barcode can be found in the URL listed in the Mirlyn record: http://dev-linux.umdl.umich.edu/cgi/pt?id=mdp.39015031699849

 

MyMBooks BookMarklet

Bookmarklet link: Save to My MBooks

To save the bookmarklet to your toolbar/links list:

Internet Explorer

  1. Right click (or control + click on mac) on the "Save to My MBooks" link above
  2. Select "Add to Favorites"
  3. When prompted "You are adding a favorite that may not be safe. Do you want to continue?" answer "Yes"
  4. Select "Create In" and highlight "Links"
  5. Click on "OK""

Firefox

  1. Left click (or just click on mac) on "Save to My MBooks" link above and drag to your toolbar
or
  1. Right click (or control + click on mac) on "Save to My MBooks" link above
  2. Select "Bookmark this Link"
  3. Select "Create in" "Bookmarks Toolbar"
  4. Click "OK"
